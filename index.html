What is Spark&Heal?

Spark&Heal is a holistic program created by a breast cancer survivor, for breast cancer survivors. It combines a free self-management course, a supportive community and live events to help you navigate survivorship with more ease.

1

Free Course

Get started with our free Self-Management Tools for Survivors course. Learn simple practices you can use every day to support your mind and body.

2

Community

Join a private, online space where you can connect with other survivors who truly understand what you are going through.

3

Events

Attend live sessions, workshops and conversations designed to help you feel more supported in every part of your survivorship.

Everything you need to know about Spark&Heal.

Spark&Heal was created by Inna Shchepanska, a breast cancer survivor, patient advocate and health coach. After navigating her own diagnosis and treatment, Inna wanted to build a program that gives survivors practical tools and a community where they feel truly seen and supported.

Our model goes beyond one-off support groups. Spark&Heal weaves together a free course, an ongoing community and live events, so you are not left to figure everything out on your own between appointments. The focus is on practical, whole-person tools that fit real life.
